Diamond Painting Fill the grid · Reveal the art

Diamond Painting is a relaxing puzzle game where you place glittering diamonds cell by cell to reveal beautiful pixel-art masterpieces.

How to Play

  • 💎Tap a cell to place a diamond
  • ✋Drag to pan the canvas
  • 🤏Pinch or scroll to zoom
